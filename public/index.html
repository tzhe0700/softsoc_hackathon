Collaborative Story Game

Create a game, share the ID, take turns adding sentences.

How to Play

  1. Click Create. Copy the shown Game ID.
  2. Share the Game ID with friends (or open this page in another tab).
  3. Each player joins with a unique Player ID and Name.
  4. When Status shows active and it’s your turn, submit a sentence.
  5. Turns rotate automatically. Watch the story grow below.
